Plot Summary

My Hero Academia is set in a world where humanity lives within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from Titans, giant humanoid creatures who devour humans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends, who join the military to battle the Titans after their hometown is destroyed. As they face relentless enemies, they uncover the dark secrets of their world and the origins of the Titans.
